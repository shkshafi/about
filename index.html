Security Professional

My proficiency is underscored by successful demonstration of application security best practices to developers, fostering seamless communication with engineering and IT teams. Leveraging hands-on experience with diverse tools such as Checkmarx, SonarCube, Docker Scout, Qualys, Nessus, Cymulate, BitSight, BurpSuite, ZAP, Nmap, Mimikatz, EnCase, FTK, Volatility and Pluggable Transport, I stay at the forefront of cybersecurity, DevSecOps and digital forensics.

Beyond cybersecurity, I boast a track record of developing dynamic web applications using advanced frameworks like ReactJs and Laravel and creating iOS mobile applications using Swift. My programming skills encompass ReactJs, HTML, SQL, Swift (iOS) and JavaScript.

Notably, I assisted my organization in obtaining CERT-IN and CREST accreditation, demonstrating my commitment to enhancing cybersecurity standards. Holding certifications as an Offensive Security Certified Professional (OSCP), Certified Red Team Professional (CRTP) and Certified Information Security Manager (CISM), I approach information security holistically. My technical expertise is complemented by a Master's in Business Management (Marketing/IT) and a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Information Technology (CSIT).
